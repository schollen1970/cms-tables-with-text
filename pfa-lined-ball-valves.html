Industrial PFA-Lined Ball Valves

PFA lined ball valves:

 

Direct dimensional replacement valves for your Atomac (Flowserve), Xomox (Crane), and/or ITT Richter industrial PFA lined ball valves - available in sizes 1” thru 6” from stock with full port design and bubble-tight shut-off.

 

Design features includes a separate ball and stem for reduced packing side-loading, anti-blowout stem, heavy-duty PFA liner resists cracking, dovetail locking grooves positively secures PFA liner into ductile iron body casting, minimum cavity-filled space surrounds ball (to reduce product accumulation and contamination issues), ASME B16.10 specs with a universal ISO 5211 mounting pad for interchangeable actuation/top works, lockable handle with static ground spring, and optional relief hole drilling available.

 

The temperature rating of these industrial PFA-lined ball valves range from -20. to +400 degrees Fahrenheit with pressures from full vacuum to 275.psig.
