PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) tubing

PTFE Tubing:   polytetrafluoroethylene tubing

PTFE tubing is suitable for a wide range of applications, and is perhaps the most widely used fluoropolymer available on the market today.  It is more flexible than other tubing in its class, and is chemically resistant to nearly all industrial fluids.  With a temperature range of approx. -300. to 500.deg.F, it offers the widest temperature range of the fluoropolymers.  In addition, it has excellent electrical properties and low permeability.  PTFE has a very low coefficient of friction.  May be used with standard compression fittings.
