Single (open) arch molded rubber expansion joint

Rubber Expansion Joints:

Rubber expansion joints available in diameters ranging from 1" thru 120" may be molded from any elastomer material.  Single, double and triple (open) arch versions to cover various allowable pipeline movement requirements.  Also available in a Teflon (PTFE) lined version.  Large inventories are kept to allow for immediate shipment.

 

Options include:  control rods, alternative elastomers, filled arch design, stainless steel back-up rings, double or triple arch design, and/or special bolt hole drilling patterns.  Non-standard designs are available, per custom quotation.
