Industrial Sanitary Clamp Gaskets

Sanitary Clamp Gaskets (Tri-Clamp™):

Sanitary clamp gaskets are used in process systems for the food, dairy, beverage, pharmaceutical and biotech markets. Manufactured from Buna-N, Viton®, EPDM, and silicone, as well as gaskets made with PTFE fluoropolymer. All compounds meet the criteria of the FDA and some compound formulations have also been certified and accepted by the USDA and USP Class VI certifications.
