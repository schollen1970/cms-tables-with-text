Sanitary orifice plate gaskets and blind (solid) gaskets

Orifice Plate Gaskets:
Orifice plates are used in process pipeline systems for the food, dairy, beverage, pharmaceutical and biotech industries. The products are manufactured from FDA-compliant Buna-N, EPDM, Silicone, Viton® and PTFE. Metal inserts are made of 316L stainless steel x 1/16” thick with a 1/8” hole (centered) as standard.  Optional hole sizes (and locations) are available upon request (including blank (blind) metal inserts).
