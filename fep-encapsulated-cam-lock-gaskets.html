FEP encapsulated camlock gaskets

Encapsulated CamLock Gaskets:

Combining the best qualities of two gasket materials, FEP (see below) on the exterior to handle aggressive chemical and temperature environments, coupled with the more resilient elastomer on the interior (fully encapsulated) to provide proper shape and elasticity under load. Available from 1/2” size up to 6” size camlock couplings.

Benefits of FEP (fluorinated ethylene propylene) encapsulated gaskets:

● excellent chemical resistance to most industrial chemicals;

● low coefficient of friction;

● low compression set;

● temperature range -75.deg.F to +400.deg.F;

● choice of elastomer core:  standard silicone (red or white) or Viton® (-15.deg.F low temperature rating);

● non-stick surface allows for easy and clean release;

● eliminates contamination associated with plain elastomeric gaskets;

● FDA-compliant and USP Class VI approved.
