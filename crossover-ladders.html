Industrial Crossover Ladders

Industrial crossover ladders provide a permanent crossover point for conveyor or other machinery, piping or low walls. Serrated steps of crossover ladders and platforms provide extra grip and safety, are 23-1/2” width and 7” depth each, with a standard step spacing of 10” (incline step angle is 58 degrees). Platform top is also serrated steel (semi-open; optional closed platform tops are also available if potential overhead contamination of product below is of concern), with 4” height toeboards included on each side. Legs include floor mounting pads. All welded, powder coated (safety yellow) steel construction ship knocked-down to save on freight costs (some field assembly required).

 

Uniform load capacity rating of 500 pounds

 

29-1/4” overall width (including floor mounting plates)

 

Meets ANSI A14.7 and OSHA 29 CFR 1910.24 standards.

 

Our industrial crossover ladders are available in optional hot drip galvanized steel, aluminum or 304 stainless steel construction for corrosive or cleanroom environments
