Sanitary valve stems

Tri-Clover®/Alfa Laval® valve stems are available new and/or reconditioned to save on maintenance budgets throughout the year, especially when multiple change-outs occur in process lines. Stem varieties are available in compression, divert and shut-off configurations. Elastomers available in Buna-N (-U), EPDM (-E) and fluoroelastomer (-SFY; Viton®) materials, all in FDA- and 3A-compliant rubber.

All reconditioned valve stems have been inspected and pre-tested to ensure proper function and tolerance.
