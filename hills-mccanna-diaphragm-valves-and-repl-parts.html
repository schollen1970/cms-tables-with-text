Hills-McCanna® Diaphragm Valve and Replacement Parts:

Original Hills-McCanna® diaphragm valves and OEM replacement parts for your processing needs.  Diaphragm valves provide bubble-tight shut-off (zero leakage in accordance with MSS SP-88), throttling service and streamlined (bi-directional) flow – smooth, contoured bodies are cavity-free and self-draining (when oriented properly).

 

Four body styles to fit any application:

1.)  Rubber-lined (1” thru 12” lined size) : soft rubber, hard rubber, Neoprene;

2.)  Plastic-lined (1/2” thru 8” line size) : polypropylene, PVDF (Kynar™), ETFE (Tefzel™), and PFA;

3.)  Glass-lined (1/2” thru 8” line size) : borosilicate for your glass-lined piping systems!

4.)  Unlined (1/2” thru 12” line size) : ductile iron, carbon steel, 316(L) stainless steel, Alloy 20, plus other metals available upon request.  Also available in plastic body (1/2” thru 2” size) : PVC, polypropylene, PVDF (Kynar™), and PFA.

 

Diaphragm options available:  natural rubber, EPDM (black/white), Neoprene, Hypalon, Viton™,  and PTFE.

 

End connections available: 150# ANSI flanged, threaded*, socket weld (SW)*, butt weld (BW)*   

* unlined (metal or plastic bodies only; ½” thru 2” line size).

 

Manual handwheel operated or available in a pneumatically actuated (on/off or modulating) version.
