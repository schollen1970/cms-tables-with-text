PFA Lined Plug Valves

Fully Teflon (PFA) lined plug valve offering for when 100.% drop-tight shut-off is an absolute requirement!  ASTM A216 Gr. WCB carbon steel, fully PFA lined body/flange faces with a duplex stainless steel ASTM A890 Gr. 4A (CD3MN), PFA lined plug prevents stem twisting/valve "freezing" issues of other lined plug valves when left in one posotion without cycling regularly (due to the relatively high torque requirement).

 

 

Plug valve features include:

 

thick one-piece continuous lining that fully covers all wetted surfaces of the valve body and face of flange;

360.degree sealing surface (as much as x10 greater than other lined valve designs) assures drop-tight shut-off;

omni-directional sealing, independent of line pressure;

zero cavity between body lining and plug;

in-line adjustable (to re-seat plug) without disturbing valve installation;

very cost effective vs. high alloy body materials.
