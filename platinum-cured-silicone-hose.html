Platinum cured silicone hose (PureFlex)

Platinum Cured Silicone Hose Assemblies

 

Available in two distinct styles: wire reinforced (typically for larger diameters) and polyester braid reinforced.

 

Platinum cured silicone hose offers the following features and benefits for your process flow/transfer requirements:

Extremely flexible, broad chemical compatibility; non-stick surface; CIP (clean-in-place), SIP (steam-in-place), autoclaveable and gamma radiation process sterilizeable; non-toxic; lot and batch traceable; imparts no odor nor taste; USP Class VI cert.

 

Wire reinforced (-80. to +500.deg.F)

1/2” up to 6” hose diameter (smooth bore interior; 316SS helical wire)

 

Other available exterior options:

Silicone sleeve outer cover, available with heat shrink color coding and text boxes for positive identification.

 

Available end fittings:

Sanitary ferrule (tri-clamp)  (also bevel seat, I-line and butt weld ends); NPT (M) male and (F) female; JIC; flanged and encapsulated flanged; cam & groove (cam-lock); compression fittings.
