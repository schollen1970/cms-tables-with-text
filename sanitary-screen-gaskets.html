Sanitary mesh screen gaskets

Sanitary Screen Gaskets:
Sanitary screen gaskets are used in process pipeline systems for the food, dairy, beverage, pharmaceutical and biotech industries. The products are manufactured from FDA-compliant EPDM, Buna-N, Silicone, Viton® and PTFE. Screen inserts are made of 316 stainless steel wire mesh or perforated discs.  Standard screen size is a #10 mesh, and standard perforated disc size are # .033 dia. holes. Optional mesh sizes, perforated disc hole sizes and materials are available upon request.

Screen: (#10 mesh) .025 dia. wire; width of opening between wires .075; O/A 56.8%; 100 openings/sqr.in.  other screen mesh sizes available upon quotation   Costs updated 10/1/2022
