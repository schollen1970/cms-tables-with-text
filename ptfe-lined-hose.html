PTFE Lined Convoluted Hose (PureFlex)

PTFE Lined Hose Assemblies

 

Available in two distinct styles : smooth bore (typically for smaller diameters) and convoluted (typically larger diameters and/or higher required flexibility).

 

PTFE offers the following features and benefits for your process flow/transfer  requirements:  extremely broad chemical compatibility; non-stick surface; FDA-approval; CIP (clean-in-place) and SIP (steam-in-place); (black) conductive liner optional, where available.

 

Smooth Bore (-65. to +450.deg.F)

1/8” up to 1-1/4” hose diameter, with stainless steel exterior braiding (std.)

 

Other available exterior options:

Polypropylene exterior braiding, Kynar exterior braiding, silicone sleeve outer cover, bare hose (no exterior braid or cover), available with heat shrink color coding and/or text boxes for positive identification.

 

Available end fittings:

NPT(M) male and (F) female; JIC; sanitary ferrule (tri-clamp) (also bevel seat, I-line and butt weld ends); flanged and flare-thru flanged (316SS or higher alloy available); cam & groove (cam-lock); compression fittings.
