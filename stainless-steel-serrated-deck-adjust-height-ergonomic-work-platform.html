Serrated deck ergonomic adjustable height worker/operator platform

Stainless Steel Serrated Deck Adjustable Height Ergonomic Work Steps / Platforms:

 

Lightweight enough to be mobile/portable, yet heavy duty enough for continuous worker/operator use.  Individual legs are adjustable within stated height range by screwing them in or out (for uneven floors or work areas) to maintain the perfect ergonomic height.  Serrated decking provides traction for wet and/or wash-down environments.  Legs are equipped with a no-slip crutch style (replaceable) tip.  500.lbs. uniform load capacity rating.  All 304 stainless steel construction, pre-fabricated/fully welded.
