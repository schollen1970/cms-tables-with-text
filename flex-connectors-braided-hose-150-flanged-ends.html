Metal Hose / Braided Flex Connectors:  ANSI 150# Flanged End Connections  typically In Stock

Carbon steel (non-rotating) ANSI 150# drilled plate flange ends with corrugated 321 stainless steel metal hose (interior) and single 304 stainless steel braid (exterior).

Braided flexible metal hose connectors are designed to control vibration, reduce noise, relieve stress, prevent system shock and compensate for lateral misalignment and movement. Use braided metal hose connectors for applications such as, but not limited to: pumps, compressors, fans and other mechanical equipment to enhance overall system operation.

﻿Flexible metal hose by itself, when subjected to high pressure, can over-extend past the ability to operate properly and safely. It is necessary, therefore, to include an external flexible restraint (braiding) to withstand the specified internal pressure and prevent excessive elongation. Woven metal braiding is designed to fit snugly over the internal corrugated metal hose, and is fastened to the ends of both hose and fittings. This braiding is designed to be strong enough to withstand elongation for the full pressure rating of the internal hose.
