Stainless Steel Serrated Deck Adjustable Ergonomic Worker / Operator Steps / Platform

 

Use as either a worker/operator platform or as a semi-permanent, yet mobile, step.  Grip strut deck surface ensures a safe, non-slip surface, even in wet and/or washdown environments.  Uniform load capacity rating of 500.lbs.  Each leg individually adjustable to allow for a level worker surface, even on uneven floors.  11-3/4” step depth each.  All 304 stainless steel construction, pre-fabricated/fully welded.  Also available in powder coated steel and aluminum construction, and with built-in handrail.
