Teflon Lined Inline Pipe Strainer (Ethylene)

Teflon Lined Inline Pipe Strainer:

Fully Teflon lined strainer delivers the ultimate chemical resistance with maximum flow rating while protecting expensive downstream equipment (pumps, heat exchangers, control valves, etc.) from potential solids/debris damage.

 

Fully lined with ETFE (standard, to +300.deg.F) or optional PFA with a solid, one-piece robust removable PTFE basket.  Rated up to 150.psig @ +400.deg.F, full vacuum rated.  Available in 2” to 8” line sizes; ANSI 150# flanged end connections.

 

Options include: basket hole sizes (1/8”, 1/4”, 1/2” or custom sized basket holes/drilling), strainer stand, fully lined drain elbow, flush basket (open) bottom design.
