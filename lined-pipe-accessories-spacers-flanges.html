Lined Pipe Accessories:  Spacers and Flanges

 

Our industrial pipe accessories include industrial spacers and industrial pipe flanges that are available in virgin Teflon (PTFE), natural unpigmented Kynar® (PVDF), natural unpigmented polypropylene (PPL), and CPVC materials. The styles include ring spacers, full face spacers, reducing tapered spacers, blind flanges, orifice spacers, tapered bore spacers, armored spacers and spectacle blinds. We have industrial pipe accessories for line sizes ½” thru 12” (and larger custom sizes) available. ANSI Class 150, 300, and ISO (metric) flange drillings available.
