Corrosion Resistant Stainless Steel Roller Conveyor

Corrosion resistant stainless steel roller gravity conveyor for washdown duty (food, beverage, pharmaceutical manufacturing, misc. corrosive environments) and other harsh environments.  Conveyor materials available in 304 and 316 stainless steel (frame and rollers), PVC (rollers) and UHMW engineered plastic components.  Roller diameters based on load (capacity) requirements and product footprint size: 3/4" o.d., 1-1/2" o.d., 1.9" o.d., with a variety of bearings options to fit the exact application.  Available in mill finish or various degrees of polished finish.  Built-to-order per application with quick leadtimes.
