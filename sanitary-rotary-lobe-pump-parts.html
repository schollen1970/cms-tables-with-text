Repl. Sanitary Rotary Lobe Pump Parts (Rotors):

 

New, reconditioned and exchange-program rubber-coated rotors for APV/Crepaco® R-series and Alfa Laval/Tri-Clover® PR-series pumps. The lobes under the rubber surface are made of either a cast stainless steel or cast aluminum core. By using a stainless steel or an aluminum base, the risk of cracking a rotor is eliminated.  A bonding agent is applied when molding rubber to the metal cores - this bonding process increases the life expectancy of the rotors and it reduces the risk of rubber pieces coming loose during production.  Available in Buna-N, EPDM and Viton (SFY/FKM) rubber - can also supply rotors coated in a metal-detectable Buna, EPDM and Viton (SFY/FKM) rubber. Rubber is 3A 18-03 certified.  Other replacement “soft” parts and seal kits are available.
