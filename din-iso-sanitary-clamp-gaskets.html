Sanitary DIN / ISO Gaskets:
DIN #32676 and ISO #2852 hygienic standard flanged sanitary clamp gaskets in both FDA compliant and USP Class VI compounds: EPDM, Silicone and Viton® rubber.  Compounds are specifically formulated for pharmaceutical and biotech companies requiring the highest hygienic standards and chemical purity.  Each component will carry a batch number and cure date making every gasket 100.% traceable.
